### Specification
- All parts are handmade (Wing material : Foamboard) & 3D-printed
- A-tailed Standard VTOL with 4 lift motors and 1 pusher motor
- Wingspan : 1.5m
- Flight Controller : Pixhawk 6X
- Firmware : PX4-Autopilot v1.14
